Welcome to the Tailr.ai application hub. Share a few details about yourself and your project so we can learn how Tailr.ai can help.

## What to include

- Your name and the best way to contact you
- A brief overview of your brand or business
- The goals you want Tailr.ai to support (launching a product line, scaling production, creative collaboration, etc.)
- Any deadlines or key dates we should know about

## Next steps

Email your details to [info@tailr.ai](mailto:info@tailr.ai) or attach them in your preferred format. We will review your application and follow up with the next steps.

Looking forward to building with you.
